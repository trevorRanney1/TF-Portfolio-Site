Trevor Furtado – Computer Science Student

    All I knew I wanted in High School was to be in the Computer Science shop. At the time I simply wanted to be able to program video games at my own will, but now I know that Computer Science is a lot more than that, and I see it as a likely possibility for a life-long career. My experience in the Old Colony Regional Vocational Technical High School has left me with a much deeper understanding of Computer Science as a concept in the real world, and I feel that I am ready to use that knowledge.
